Research Interests

The fast computation of Riemann Roch spaces has a particular interest in coding code theory and cryptography. My research focus on the computation of bases of Riemann-Roch spaces with arithmetical method. Therefore, I am interested in divisors of function field, integral bases of fractional ideals and their reduction.
